SNJB (Jain Gurukul)

SNJB located at Neminagar, Chandwad a taluka place which is part of Nashik district in Maharastra, is an Educational Institute. It had a humble beginning in the year 1928 with just three students. Today it has grown up in big stature. Since its establishment, SNJB has been achieving greater heights wih a quest for excellence.

The qualified academic and administrive staffs of 900 hvae a pivotal role carrying out the aims, mission and objectives of the institutes.
